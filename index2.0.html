About Me:

Hi, I’m Geoff! I’m a Chef turned Web Developer! I've learned modern web programming languages and best practices from HackerYou as a graduate of their Immersive Web Development Bootcamp.

I love and excel working in highly collaborative environments as talking to my computer alone is frowned upon. The move from cooking to coding has been an amazing journey. Being a Chef and working in kitchens has taught me skills that make me a great developer such as constant self improvement and problem solving on the fly.

When I'm not coding, I can be found playing Magic the Gathering, singing Disney songs, or playing video games. I’ve also recently taking an interest in Ice Fishing.
